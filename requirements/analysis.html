Analysis of Licensing of the Data Source

  1. Publisher and the rightsholder

    2. The publisher and rightsholder of the two datasets that we use is the Ayuntamiento de Madrid.

  2. License of the data source

    3. The data sets have an open license, which permits their comercial as well as non-commercial use, provided that such use does not constitute a public administrative activity. This authorized reuse of the data includes activities such as copying, disseminating, modifying, adapting, extracting, rearranging and combining information.

    Original Spanish text: "Las presentes condiciones generales permiten la reutilización de los documentos y datos sometidos a ellas para fines comerciales y no comerciales. Se entiende por reutilización el uso de documentos que obran en poder del Ayuntamiento de Madrid y de los demás organismos y entidades adscritos a él, por personas físicas o jurídicas, con fines comerciales o no comerciales, siempre que dicho uso no constituya una actividad administrativa pública. Por ejemplo, la reutilización autorizada incluye actividades como la copia, difusión, modificación, adaptación, extracción, reordenación y combinación de la información."

    For more information, please refer to: Datos Abiertos Madrid - Condiciones de Uso

  3. License of the generated dataset

    4. The generated data set can also be published with an open license that permits the comercial as well as non-comercial use. However, the general conditions for reuse must be taken into account when publishing the generated dataset.

    1. It is forbidden to distort the meaning of the information.
    2. The source of the documents to be reused must be cited. This quotation may be made as follows: "Origin of the data: Madrid City Council (or, as the case may be, the administrative body, body or entity in question)".
    3. The date of the last update of the documents to be reused must be mentioned, as long as it was included in the original document.
    4. It may not indicate, imply or suggest that Madrid City Council participates in, sponsors or supports the reuse carried out with the information.
    5. The metadata on the date of update and the applicable conditions for reuse included, where applicable, in the document made available for reuse must be kept, not altered or deleted.
    6. In the case of anonymised information due to personal data protection or other reasons, it is expressly forbidden to re-identify people on the basis of these data and other possible, past, current or future sources of data and information.

    For more information, please refer to: Datos Abiertos Madrid - Condiciones generales para la reutilización

Analysis of Resource Naming

For the resource naming, we mainly - except for one case - rely on existing ontologies and their naming conventions. We made use of the existing schema.org, dbpedia and vcard vocabularies in order to name the entities and properties that are present in the dataset.

Please also refer to the visualization of our ontology for an overview.

  1. Our domain: http://www.semanticweb.org/museumsandmonumentsmadrid
  2. Examples of our class URIs (used from existing ontologies): http://dbpedia.org/ontology/Place, http://dbpedia.org/ontology/Monument, http://schema.org/CivicStructure, http://schema.org/Museum
  3. Examples of our object property URIs (mainly used from existing ontologies): http://www.semanticweb.org/museumsandmonumentsmadrid/reachableby, http://schema.org/address
  4. Examples of our data property URIs (used from existing ontologies): http://schema.org/addressLocality, http://schema.org/name, http://www.w3.org/2006/vcard/ns#email
